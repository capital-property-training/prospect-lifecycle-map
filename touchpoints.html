What's counted: every contact that reaches the prospect — automated emails/SMS plus manual WhatsApp and calls. "Calls" includes phone chases and the Zoom sales call. Purchased course carries the 5-email sequence; Call booked carries 3 emails (confirmation + 2 reminders).
Not counted: Slack team alerts, the email notification to you, retargeting ads, and the Skool system invite.
Conditional steps: some SOP calls/WhatsApps only fire if an earlier contact gets no reply — these are counted at their maximum, so real-world totals are often lower.
